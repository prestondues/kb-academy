# KB Academy Training Records Page

## Purpose
Provides a dedicated page to review training session history outside of the main Training dashboard.

## Current Capabilities
- view completed and in-progress training sessions
- filter by trainee
- filter by trainer
- filter by module
- filter by status
- view duration and completion time
- open session detail

## Future Expansion
- date range filters
- export
- department and shift filters
- certification status filter
- skill gap reporting
